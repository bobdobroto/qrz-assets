I got my first license (only VHF/UHF up to 5W) as LZ1BOD in 1997, but due to various reasons I wasn`t very active on the air. I renewed my license in 2015 (again as LZ1BOD) and since 2022 I can already be heard on HF as LZ1BO.

I love traveling around my beautiful country, hiking mountains, working outdoors and experimenting with different antennas at different places. That`s why I prefer QRP, wire antennas, SOTA/POTA/F&F activations and good propagation.

Occasionally when the weather is not suitable for outdoor activities or when the propagation is weak I have fun with FT8 digi mode....but I don't like it....prefer a human voice, the noise of the universe and the difficulty to get the other's callsign or report.

In the summer, I participate in VHF/UHF contests with friends as a tim. I have no experience in HF contests yet, but that will change soon ;)

Many thanks to everyone who answered my QSO calls and I look forward to meeting everyone else for a summit on the air.

LZ1BO, 73
